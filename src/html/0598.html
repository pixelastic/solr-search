parse.

Grouping by Query
In this example, we will use the group.query parameter to find the top three results for "memory" in two
different price ranges: 0.00 to 99.99, and over 100.
http://localhost:8983/solr/techproducts/select?indent=true&fl=name,price&q=memory&group=true
&group.query=price:0+TO+99.99&group.query=price:[100+TO+*]&group.limit=3
